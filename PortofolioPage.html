What is TrendyMan?

TrendyMan is a personalized styling service for men: we propose a new solution to men who want to be well dressed without having to do shopping.Frankly, for most of us, shopping is often a chore, either online or in stores. It's thinking about the uses and the specific needs of men than we imagined TrendyMan.At your request, your stylist composes a personalized selection of clothing from the brands you love and makes it delivered to your door. You have 7 days to try the clothes, without any obligation to purchase.You pay only the clothes you keep, we get the rest. Shipping and returns are free.


TrendyMan is for men who want to wear quality clothes of the best brands, but do not have time or do not like shopping, online or in stores. The service is particularly relevant for those of us who do not have an urgent need to buy clothes or who are not looking for a specific piece for personalized composition.
